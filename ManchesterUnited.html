#3 Manchester United

The Manchester United football club is worth $3.69 billion.

Manchester United Football Club is a professional football club based in Old Trafford, Greater Manchester, England. Manchester United plays in the Premier League.

Manchester United have won a record 20 League titles, 12 FA Cups, 5 League Cups and a record 21 FA Community Shields. The club has also won 3 UEFA Champions Leagues, 1 UEFA Europa League, 1 UEFA Cup Winners' Cup, 1 UEFA Super Cup, 1 Intercontinental Cup and 1 FIFA Club World Cup.
